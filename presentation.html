002 — Motivation

The Invisible Problem

Two people performing a plank can look visually identical — same alignment, same hold — while engaging completely different muscle groups. A coach watching from outside cannot distinguish these strategies.

This extends to hand manipulation, sports, and rehabilitation — where internal muscle recruitment determines everything from injury risk to motor learning.

Same movement. Different muscles. Invisible.

Person B

003 — Current Landscape

What Current Sensors See

Vision

✓ Surface geometry, joint angles, posture

✗ Cannot see beneath the skin

EMG

✓ Electrical muscle activation

✗ Crosstalk, poor depth resolution

Tactile

✓ Contact forces, pressure distribution

✗ Cannot identify which muscles caused it

None see the internal mechanical reality of movement.

004 — The Solution

Ultrasound Sees the Engine

Real-time visualization of sub-surface tissue dynamics — causally upstream of both movement and force.

  • ▸ Muscle deformation — individual muscles shortening and changing shape in real time
  • ▸ Tendon sliding — tendons gliding through sheaths as fingers move
  • ▸ Deep muscle activation — buried muscles invisible to surface EMG
  • ▸ Fascicle architecture — fiber geometry that determines force capacity

Seeing the engine, not just the tire tracks.

B-mode forearm ultrasound • real-time • wearable

005 — Research Gaps

Three Gaps This Research Fills

01

No Sensor Correlation Study

No work has rigorously quantified how ultrasound correlates with and complements EMG, vision, force, and motion capture across all meaningful combinations.

02

No Reasoning Benchmark

MMTouch demonstrated multi-tactile reasoning power (+18.9% gain), but no benchmark includes ultrasound — the only modality revealing internal muscle dynamics.

03

No AI Training System

Current rehab and sports AI relies on external cameras only. No system uses ultrasound’s internal visibility for real-time, personalized training guidance.

006 — Research Plan

3-Year Research Path

YEAR 1

UltraPose

Multimodal sensor correlation study. 15–25 subjects, controlled hand poses, exhaustive cross-modal ablation.

NeurIPS D&B Dataset Release

YEAR 2

Reasoning Benchmark

MMTouch-style benchmark with ultrasound. Free-form manipulation, multimodal model training, modality ablation.

CVPR / NeurIPS Benchmark

YEAR 3

Agentic AI Coach

Real-time training system using US+EMG+Vision. Movement correctness at the internal muscle level, personalized feedback.

Nature-family CHI / UIST

007 — Impact

Broader Impact

Rehabilitation

Real-time muscle-level feedback for stroke recovery, post-surgical rehab, and neuromuscular disorders. “Your deep flexor is at 30% — engage it more.”

Sports Training

Proactive injury prevention by detecting internal recruitment errors before they cause harm. From reactive treatment to proactive optimization.

Research Community

Open datasets and benchmarks catalyzing ultrasound-inclusive multimodal research — as emg2pose did for EMG and MMTouch for tactile.
